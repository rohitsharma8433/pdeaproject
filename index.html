Tom And Jerry

Tom and Jerry is a timeless animated series featuring the humorous rivalry between Tom, a cat, and Jerry, a clever mouse. Their slapstick chases, clever tricks, and minimal dialogue create universal appeal. Created by Hanna and Barbera in 1940, the show remains an iconic masterpiece in animation history.
